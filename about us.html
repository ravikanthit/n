About Us

ONE STEP PHYSICS is established by a group of young Technocrat, who decided to provide a platform for preparation of national level engineering and Medical entrance examination. ONE STEP PHYSICS is a team of young , dynamic member and every member of the team is vowed and dedicated for the cause of individual’s qualitative development to achieve his mission in life. We have all faculties from IIT’s and NIT’s and every member of the institute is dedicated towards betterment of each and every students.

Our Core Values
  • Our institute has very positive environment which motivate students to take their goal very seriously.
  • We also provide counselling to each and every student regarding all kind of problems.
  • Topic wise and combined test are conducted regularly to check the level of preparation for exams.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To impart quality education to achieve excellence in engineering and Medical exam along with equal attention on fundamental concepts.

To create an environment conducive to creativity ,innovation and entrepreneurial.

Vision

Values

Academic excellence and integrity, Outstanding teaching and service Integration of teaching, service Individual and collective excellence Education of individuals across the life span, Collegiality and collaboration.
